From: Arjun Mehta <arjun@vertexia.com>
To: all@vertexia.com
Date: Monday, April 10, 2023, 8:02 AM PT
Subject: Leadership Update — Sarah Chen promoted to CTO; Engineering reorg
------------------------------------------------------------

Team,

I'm thrilled to share that Sarah Chen has been promoted from VP Engineering to Chief Technology Officer, effective today.

Sarah has been with Vertexia since the very beginning. As our first engineering hire, then VP Engineering, she has built the team that shipped NexusFlow, InsightLens, and PulseConnect, led us through the DataCraft integration last year, and quietly held things together through Project Phoenix. She has more than earned this seat.

Alongside Sarah's promotion, we're restructuring Engineering into two organizations to support our scale:

  • Platform Engineering — led by Marcus Webb. Includes Data Platform (Priya Nair) and Security (Daniel Osei).
  • Product Engineering — led by Tomás García, who is being elevated from Director to Lead of Product Engineering. Includes the NexusFlow (Yuki Tanaka), InsightLens (Aisha Johnson), and PulseConnect (Raj Patel) teams.

Both Marcus and Tomás will report directly to Sarah.

Please join me in congratulating Sarah and Tomás. Cake in the SF kitchen at 3pm; Austin and Bangalore, we'll send something your way.

Arjun

Hi all,

Quick but very happy note: Aisha Johnson has been promoted to Senior Manager, effective this pay period. She continues to lead the InsightLens team.

Since taking over InsightLens last year, Aisha has delivered the v3 dashboard rewrite, brought InsightLens to $1.4M in Q1 revenue, and built one of the most cohesive squads in the org. Her partnership with Yuki's NexusFlow team on the shared events pipeline has been a model for how we want product teams to collaborate.

Congrats, Aisha — well deserved.

Tomás
