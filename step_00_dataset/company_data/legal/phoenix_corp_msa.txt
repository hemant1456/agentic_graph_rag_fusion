MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT: SUMMARY OF TERMS
Vertexia Inc. / Phoenix Corp.
Effective Date: June 1, 2022

1. PARTIES AND SCOPE
This Master Service Agreement ("MSA") is entered into by and between Vertexia Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business in San Francisco, CA (“Vertexia”), and Phoenix Corp. (“Client”). This agreement governs the provision of NexusFlow, InsightLens, and PulseConnect services as defined in the attached Statement of Work (SOW).

2. SERVICE AVAILABILITY AND PERFORMANCE
Vertexia warrants that the Services shall be available to the Client with a Monthly Uptime Percentage of at least 99.99% (“SLA”). This SLA is a critical performance obligation for the duration of the contract term. 

For the purposes of this agreement, “Monthly Uptime Percentage” is calculated by subtracting from 100% the percentage of 1-minute intervals during the monthly billing cycle in which the Services were in a state of “Unavailable.” Unavailability does not include scheduled maintenance performed with at least 72 hours’ notice.

3. DOWNTIME CREDITS
In the event Vertexia fails to meet the 99.99% uptime SLA, the Client shall be entitled to a service credit. For every 0.01% (or fraction thereof) that the Monthly Uptime Percentage falls below the 99.99% threshold, Vertexia shall issue a credit equal to 10% of the monthly fee for the affected service. Such credits are the Client's sole and exclusive remedy for any breach of the Service Availability SLA. Requests for credits must be submitted in writing to the Vertexia Finance Department within 30 days of the month in which the failure occurred.

4. CONTRACT TERM
The initial term of this Agreement shall be three (3) years, commencing on June 1, 2022, and ending on May 31, 2025. This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year terms unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least 90 days prior to the expiration of the current term.

5. DATA PROCESSING AND SECURITY
Vertexia shall process all Client data in accordance with the standard Vertexia Data Processing Addendum (DPA) and the following storage parameters:
- Hot Storage: 90 days of customer data.
- Cold Storage: 1 year of customer data (S3 Glacier).
- Log Retention: 30 days.
- Backup Frequency: Daily snapshots with 30-day retention.

6. DEDICATED SUPPORT
Client is entitled to the “Enterprise Tier” support package. This includes access to a dedicated Customer Success Manager, 24/7/365 incident response, and a priority queue for all technical support tickets. Technical escalations regarding platform stability will be routed directly to the Product Engineering team lead.

7. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.
