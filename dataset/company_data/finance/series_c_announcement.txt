### Series C FAQ

**Why are we raising this round now?**
Despite the challenging market, our growth remains strong. With $16.5M in ARR as of the end of Q3 2023, we have proven product-market fit. This funding ensures we have the runway to double down on our enterprise strategy and capture more market share.

**What are the specific use-of-proceeds?**
1. **International Expansion:** Scaling our operations in Bangalore and exploring new regional hubs.
2. **AI Features:** Investing in the research and development of generative capabilities across our product suite.
3. **Enterprise Sales:** Expanding our sales headcount to support the growth of key accounts, similar to the scale of our Phoenix Corp partnership.

**How does this impact our financial reporting?**
While our growth is strong, we remain disciplined. As of Q3 2023, we reported $4.12M in GAAP revenue against $4.2M in bookings. Moving forward, Robert Okafor and the finance team will continue to provide transparency regarding these metrics as we scale.

**Does this change our product roadmap?**
Our core focus remains on the NexusFlow, InsightLens, and PulseConnect pillars. We are doubling down on stability—especially following the learnings from the NexusFlow v2.1 outage on August 14—to ensure we continue meeting the 99.99% uptime SLA requirements for our enterprise customers like Phoenix Corp.

**Who led the round?**
Sequoia Capital led the investment, joined by our existing investors. Their belief in our vision for data-driven customer engagement further validates the hard work of the entire engineering, product, and go-to-market teams.
